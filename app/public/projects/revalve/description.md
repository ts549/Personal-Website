Revalve is a cross-market valuation engine for secondary/resale goods. You hand it a product — a sneaker, a trading card, a watch, anything resold — and it returns a fair market value, market metrics, and a buy/hold/sell signal based on realtime marketplace data. The goal is to have the knowledge of an expert pawn shop owner in the palm of your hands.

It is built for resellers, collectors, or even the average consumer who need to answer "is this priced right?" on anything they buy. This is done through a Chrome extension, so it is directly integrated into a user's shopping experience.
